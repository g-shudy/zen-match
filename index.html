How to Play

Basics

Swap adjacent gems to make rows or columns of 3 or more matching colors. Drag a gem onto its neighbor, or click one gem then click an adjacent gem to swap.

After a match clears, gems fall and new ones fill in from the top. If those create new matches, they chain automatically for bonus points.

Special Gems

4 in a row
Creates a Bomb that explodes a 3×3 area when matched.
T or L shape
Also creates a Bomb.
5 in a row
Creates a Line gem that clears its entire row or column.
6+ in a group
Creates a Rainbow that clears all gems of a color when swapped.

Combining Specials

Swap two special gems together for powerful combos. Bomb+Bomb makes a bigger explosion. Line+Line clears a full cross. Rainbow+Bomb explodes every gem of that color.

Scoring

Each gem cleared is worth 10 points. Longer matches and special gems add bonuses. Cascading chains multiply your score.

The Avg/Move display tracks your average points per move. Use the slider to change the number of gem colors.
